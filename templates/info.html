Instruction step 1/2:

You will be presented a total of 15 movies. Most of these movies were produced during 1990-2009. This survey should take around 10 minutes. Take your time to rate them using the following steps please:

  1. Look at the cover and the title. If you have seen this movie, click on the switch below "seen" so that it turns blue.
  2. If you have not seen the movie, press the symbol below "more info." This will open a webpage with extra information about the film.
  3. Once you have enough information about the movie, rate it using the scale below. 5 is perfect and 0.5 is awful
  4. Press the "submit" button once and wait until the next movie appears.
